Designed a tri-fold product pamphlet for a skincare brand using Adobe InDesign and Illustrator. The brief called for something that felt premium yet approachable — with clean layouts, consistent typography and purposeful white space.

The result was a pamphlet that increased walk-in inquiries by 28% at the brand's retail location. The design system created for this project was later extended to packaging and social media.
